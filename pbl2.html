Family Laws in India

• Family law in India is largely governed by personal laws that differ by religion (e.g. Hindu, Muslim, Christian, and Parsi laws).
• These laws cover marriage, divorce, adoption, custody, maintenance, inheritance, and other personal matters.
• There is ongoing debate and gradual reform toward a Uniform Civil Code to harmonize these diverse laws.
